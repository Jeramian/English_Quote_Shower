School Smarts or Street Smarts?

“A wise man ‘ud take en buil’ a biler-factry”(58).

Street Smarts!

This could be a connection to people like opportunists who use what is given to them to make something bigger. Like Jim said if he had that many wives he would put them to work and make money.

“Oho-o! I think I see. You want to SELL all your property to me not give it. That’s the correct idea”(18).

School Smarts!

Judge Thatcher uses his knowledge of the legal system here, By selling his property to the Judge, Huck has made a legal transaction that is upheld by the law. This money is now owned and protected by Judge Thatcher

“All right, then, I'll go to hell”(217).

Street Smarts!
